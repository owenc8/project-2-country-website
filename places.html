Sightseeing

Must visit places

Sentosa Island

A popular island resort with a variety of attractions including beaches, theme parks, and nature trails.

Gardens by the Bay

A futuristic park featuring the iconic Supertree Grove, Cloud Forest, and Flower Dome.

Merlion Park

Home to the iconic Merlion statue, a symbol of Singapore's heritage and identity.

Buddha Tooth Relic Temple

A stunning temple in Chinatown that houses a sacred relic of the Buddha.

Chinatown

A vibrant area filled with traditional shops, markets, and eateries.

Singapore Zoo

A world-renowned zoo known for its open-concept exhibits and diverse animal species.
